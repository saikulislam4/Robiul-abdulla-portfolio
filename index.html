I'm Robiul Abdulla, a passionate motion designer, 2D animator, video editor, and Visual designer with over 12 years of experience crafting captivating visuals and bringing stories to life through movement, editing, and design. How I Gained Vast Experience in Post-Production:

My career began at Film Production House, where I spent eight years as an assistant Director, Coordinator, and Video Editor cum Motion designer. I worked on films, TV commercials, and dramas, immersing myself in the full spectrum of video production and design. This hands-on experience helped me develop a deep understanding of the creative process, from ideation and scriptwriting to post-production to the outcome.
To further enhance my skills, I completed a Diploma in 3D Animation and VFX, which broadened my expertise and added another dimension to my motion design and graphic design capabilities. My comprehensive experience allows me to deliver high-quality, creative projects while navigating tight deadlines and managing complex workflows.

My Recent Role as Creative Director:

Currently, I serve as the Creative Team Lead at Mojaru Education Technologies Ltd., where I oversee the entire video production process. My role involves conceptualizing, scriptwriting, filming, editing, graphic designing, and ensuring final deliveries align with our educational goals and brand guidelines. I lead and manage a team of dedicated video editors, motion designers, and graphic designers, coordinating efforts to maintain high standards of creativity and visual consistency across all projects.
Collaboration with other departments is key to my role, ensuring innovation and maintaining our brand identity. My leadership focuses on motivating my team, managing timelines, meeting project deadlines, and enhancing audience engagement through visually compelling and educationally enriching content.
